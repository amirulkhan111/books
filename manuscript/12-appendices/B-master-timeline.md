# Appendix B: Master Chronological Timeline (All Periods)

*Part XII: Appendices*

---

This appendix merges every date entry from every chapter's own Timeline table across Parts I through XI into a single chronological sequence, spanning Aristotle's *Poetics* (c. 335 BCE) to the present. Entries are sorted by their earliest identifiable year; where a date is a range, century, or disputed estimate, it is placed at that range's or century's approximate start. Rows are deduplicated only where two chapters record the exact same date with the exact same wording — near-duplicates that phrase a shared event differently (as many chapters do, when citing earlier background) are kept as separate rows, each citing its own source chapter, so this table remains a complete, unabridged merge rather than a curated highlights list.

**Total entries: 1323**, drawn from the Timeline table of every chapter in Parts I–XI.
